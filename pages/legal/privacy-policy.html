Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 29.01.2025

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following data when you use Kodda:

  • Account Information: Username, email, and password (encrypted for security).
  • User-Generated Content: Snippet names, descriptions, languages, and code.

We do not collect tracking data or analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Kodda service.
  • Securely store your snippets for easy access.
  • Enable account management (e.g., login, password reset, email changes).

We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All user data is stored on a private server.
  • Passwords are encrypted before being stored.
  • We use MongoDB, hosted on a private server, to store user data securely.
  • Payments (after full release) will be handled via Stripe. Kodda does not store payment details.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Hugging Face Inference API – Used for AI-powered property generation for snippets.
  • Stripe (after release) – Used for handling payments securely.

These services have their own privacy policies, which apply when using their features.

5. User Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Delete your account (this permanently removes all your data from our servers).
  • Update your account information (change your username, email, or password).

To request data deletion, contact us at contact@kodda.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Kodda does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics to monitor user behavior.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if necessary. If changes are significant, we will notify users.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@kodda.com.

Thank you for using Kodda!
